# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect
The YouTube Batch Uploader ("the Software") collects and processes the following information:

### Authentication Data
- OAuth tokens for YouTube and TikTok accounts
- User profile information (name, email, avatar URL) from connected accounts

### Content Data
- Video files you choose to upload
- Video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, privacy settings)

### Technical Data
- Application logs for debugging purposes
- Machine code for license verification

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To facilitate video uploads to YouTube and TikTok
- To manage your connected accounts and channels
- To verify software licenses
- To improve application performance and stability

## 3. Data Storage
- All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database
- Video files are stored locally until upload is complete, after which local copies may be deleted
- We do not maintain cloud servers that store your content or personal data

## 4. Data Sharing
- We do not sell your personal data
- Video content is uploaded directly to YouTube (Google) and TikTok (ByteDance) servers through their official APIs
- Authentication is handled through Google OAuth and TikTok OAuth directly

## 5. Third-Party Services
The Software integrates with:
- **Google/YouTube**: For YouTube video uploads (privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **TikTok/ByteDance**: For TikTok video uploads (privacy policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- **License Server**: A third-party server used for license verification (no personal data transmitted)

## 6. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:
- Local encryption of stored credentials
- Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication flows
- No transmission of sensitive data to unauthorized servers

## 7. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Delete your account data from the application at any time
- Revoke OAuth permissions through Google and TikTok account settings
- Request deletion of locally stored data by uninstalling the application

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
